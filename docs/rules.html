Quizit Rules

Quizit is a question and answer game designed to test your knowledge as
a junior developer and stimulate friendly competition.

When prompted for a "player name" enter your name.

The first question will then be shown ."multiple choice"
Each multiple choice possible answer will have a letter associated with it.

To answer type the letter you think is associated to the correct answer
"a", "b","c","d" are the possible choices.

Questions are catagory based, they start easy and get progressivly harder.
With 9 questions in each of 3 different catagories, for a total of 27 questions.

You will gain 1 point for each correct answer!.

close or minimise this window to get to the game.

"Good Luck, May the force be with you!"
